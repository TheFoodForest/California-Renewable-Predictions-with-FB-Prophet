Daily solar production color coded by time of day. Solar Power production is relatively predictable depending on the time of year.

Solar power provides the largest share of total renewable energy, so the largest MWh production values for renewable energy are during the summer months.

Wind power is less predictable than solar. Generally, larger MWh production values are in the summer months in the late evening or early morning.

California Energy API

Available Routes

localhost:5000/api/renewable_prod/date/YYYY-mm-dd

Returns Renewable Production, total production, and percent of production provided by renewable sources per hour for the date provided. Optional unit parameter that can be passed at end of url as "/GWh" to return measurements in GWh.

localhost:5000/api/renewable_prod/date/range/START/END

Returns Renewable Production, total production, and percent of production provided by renewable sources for each hour of every day (with data) between START and END dates. Same optional unit parameter as single date. Date format: YYYY-mm-dd.

localhost:5000/api/renewable_demand/date/YYYY-mm-dd

Returns Renewable Production, total demand, and percent of demand met by renewables for date provided. Optional unit parameter as well.

localhost:5000/api/renewable_demand/date/range/START/END

Returns Renewable Production, total demand, and percent of demand met by renewables for date range provided. Optional unit parameter. Date format: YYYY-mm-dd.
